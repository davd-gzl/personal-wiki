# Awesome Digital Nomad

A curated list of resources for digital nomads.

## Travel & Flights

### Flight Search Engines
- [Kiwi.com](https://www.kiwi.com/) - Nomad mode to find multi-city routes and travel hacks
- [Kayak](https://www.kayak.com/) - Comprehensive flight search and comparison
- [Skyscanner](https://www.skyscanner.com/) - Find cheap flights and flexible date searches

## Community & Networking

### Finding Other Nomads
- [Nomad List](https://nomadlist.com/) - Find other digital nomads and best places to live/work

### House & Pet Sitting
